1/2 lb butter
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 cups sugar
5 eggs
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F
2. Cream butter and shortening
3. Add sugar slowly
4. Add eggs one at a time, blending after each
5. Stir dry ingredients together in separate bowl
6. Add dry ingredients alternating with milk (start and end with flour)
7. Mix in vanilla
8. Pour into greased and floured tube pan
9. Bake for 60-90 minutes until toothpick in center comes out clean
